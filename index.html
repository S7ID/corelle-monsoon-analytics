What the Data Says

💰 MER 14.8× — the store runs on demand

Blended MER (₹15.4L revenue ÷ ₹1.04L spend) is ~3× Meta's attributed 4.52×. Paid is only ~7% of revenue — an accelerant on strong organic/repeat demand. Read MER for the business; use Meta ROAS only to steer delivery.

📈 MER 5.80× campaign-to-date

₹3.80L paid revenue ÷ ₹65.5K spend over the full 10–23 Jul window. Image 4.34×, catalogue 3.39×. Healthy on a blended basis — but read the two cards below before acting on it.

🧮 Incremental MER is 1.77×, not 5.80×

The store did ₹22,304/day gross with ads OFF (19 Jun–9 Jul) and ₹30,573/day with ads ON. So paid added ~₹8,269/day incremental → ₹1.16L over 14 days on ₹65.5K spend = 1.77× incremental MER. Blended MER credits paid with baseline demand that was already there. Caveat: naive pre/post, no holdout — the gift offer and monsoon seasonality also landed on 10 Jul. But at typical dinnerware margin, 1.77× is at or below contribution breakeven. Worth a proper geo/holdout test next campaign.

📉 Both engines are decaying — this is fatigue, not noise

Blended Meta ROAS 4.61× → 3.77× over the last 7 days (image 4.74→4.07, catalogue 4.24→2.69). CTR is falling in lockstep (image 5.58%→5.00%, catalogue 5.18%→4.49%) while frequency climbs on a reach of only 64,475. Falling CTR + rising frequency + falling ROAS is the textbook fatigue signature. More budget into this audience buys worse returns — the fix is new creative and a wider audience, not a higher bid.

🎯 ₹14L target is out of reach — reset it now

₹8.96L gross booked of the ₹14L goal (19 Jun–26 Jul) = 64%. Closing the ₹5.04L gap in 3 days needs ₹1.68L/day against a current ₹30.6K/day — a 5.5× step-change. Realistic landing is ~₹9.9L (71%). Root causes are documented and not performance-side: campaign started 21 days late (ads live 10 Jul, not 19 Jun) and Google never got funded, so 30% of the planned media mix never ran. Flag to client now, not on 27 Jul.

⚠️ Morning Blue is absorbing budget it hasn't earned

It takes 57% of image spend (₹28.7K) at 3.55× — the weakest of the funded creatives despite the best CTR (6.03%). Plates does 5.96× on ₹14.1K and Maggi 5.15× on ₹4.6K. Great hook, weaker closer: high CTR pulling low-intent clicks. Move ~₹1,000/day to Plates and give Momos a real test budget.

🌱 A warm account, relaunched

Spend fell to ₹1,073 in the last 30 days on a proven 4.52× base. Monsoon Mania restarts the engine at the ideal moment — no cold-start.

📈 Fund the young

25–34 returns 8.06× on ₹4.2K while 35–44 (₹12.6K) drags at 3.18×. Shifting budget younger is the single clearest efficiency lever.

🎯 Feed first

Feed = 74% of spend at 4.93× ROAS. Keep Stories/Reels for cheap reach & CTR, but don't let them pull budget from Feed. Cut or fix FB Reels (1.57×).

📱 Weight iPhone

iPhone converts at 4.86× vs Android's 3.74×, even though Android clicks more. Value-based bidding will naturally lean iOS.

👩 Women-led

Women are 80% of spend, 8.4% CTR, 4.60× ROAS. The creative and offer are landing with the core buyer — lean in.

🔌 Close the data gap

Only Meta is wired up. Connecting Corelle's own GA4, Clarity & Shopify moves this from Meta-attributed to true blended ROAS.
