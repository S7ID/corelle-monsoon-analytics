What the Data Says

🌱 A warm account, relaunched

Spend fell to ₹1,073 in the last 30 days on a proven 4.52× base. Monsoon Mania restarts the engine at the ideal moment — no cold-start.

📈 Fund the young

25–34 returns 8.06× on ₹4.2K while 35–44 (₹12.6K) drags at 3.18×. Shifting budget younger is the single clearest efficiency lever.

🎯 Feed first

Feed = 74% of spend at 4.93× ROAS. Keep Stories/Reels for cheap reach & CTR, but don't let them pull budget from Feed. Cut or fix FB Reels (1.57×).

📱 Weight iPhone

iPhone converts at 4.86× vs Android's 3.74×, even though Android clicks more. Value-based bidding will naturally lean iOS.

👩 Women-led

Women are 80% of spend, 8.4% CTR, 4.60× ROAS. The creative and offer are landing with the core buyer — lean in.

🔌 Close the data gap

Only Meta is wired up. Connecting Corelle's own GA4, Clarity & Shopify moves this from Meta-attributed to true blended ROAS.
